## 🧭 Mission

LeafAI is an open-source AI research and engineering organization. We build **composable AI agents**, **high-performance inference servers**, and **developer tooling** that push the boundaries of what AI can do in the hands of real developers.

From CLI coding agents to real-time speech transcription, from MCP extensions for office suites to multi-agent cooperation frameworks — we ship practical AI that works.

## 🤝 Contributing

We welcome contributions across all projects! Here's how to get started:

1. **Explore** — browse our [repositories](https://github.com/orgs/LeafAI/repositories) and find one that excites you
2. **Pick an issue** — look for `good first issue` labels
3. **Fork & PR** — fork the repo, make your changes, and submit a pull request
4. **Join the discussion** — open issues for bugs, feature requests, or ideas

Most projects follow standard conventions: `cargo` for Rust, `go mod` for Go, `npm` for TypeScript/JavaScript.
